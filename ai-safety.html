AI-Powered PPE Detection & Safety Monitoring

Our AI Safety Assistant leverages advanced computer vision and machine learning to automatically detect Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) compliance and monitor safety protocols in real-time across construction sites and industrial facilities.

Advanced AI Technology

The AI Safety Assistant uses state-of-the-art deep learning models trained on thousands of construction site images to identify safety equipment and potential hazards. The system operates in real-time, providing instant alerts when safety violations are detected.

Key Capabilities

PPE Detection

Automatically identifies hard hats, safety vests, safety glasses, gloves, and safety boots on workers entering hazardous areas.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous surveillance through CCTV cameras with instant violation detection and automated alert generation.

Instant Alerts

Immediate notifications sent to supervisors and safety officers when PPE violations or unsafe conditions are detected.

Hazard Recognition

Identifies unsafe behaviors, restricted area violations, and potential hazards before incidents occur.

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive reporting with compliance rates, violation trends, and safety performance metrics.

Customizable Rules

Configure detection rules based on specific site requirements, zones, and safety protocols.
