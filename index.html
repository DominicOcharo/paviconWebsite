Who we are

About us

Pavicon (K) Limited was formally incorporated in Nairobi in July 2011 as a Limited Liability Company with an objective to provide reliable solutions in structural and civil engineering.

Over the years our unique solutions offering, expertise and trusted services have pivoted the company to now offering turnkey solutions in telecommunication, civil works and construction, ICT, power and energy sectors.

With a regional presence across Kenya, South Sudan, Ethiopia, Malawi, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania, Mauritius, Botswana & Zambia, we have successfully delivered over 10,000 projects in the region.

Building strong structures. Powering them with smart technology. Pavicon Group delivers infrastructure built for today and ready for tomorrow.

Our Project Delivery Approach

Process

A systematic 5-phase methodology ensuring successful project execution

1

Initiation & Planning

We begin by thoroughly understanding project requirements, conducting feasibility studies, and developing comprehensive project plans. This phase includes stakeholder engagement, resource allocation, and risk assessment to ensure a solid foundation for success.

  • Requirements Analysis
  • Feasibility Studies
  • Project Planning
  • Risk Assessment
2

Design & Engineering

Our expert engineers develop detailed designs, technical specifications, and engineering drawings. We ensure all designs comply with relevant standards and regulations while optimizing for cost-effectiveness and sustainability.

  • Technical Design
  • Engineering Drawings
  • Standards Compliance
  • Cost Optimization
3

Procurement & Mobilization

We source quality materials and equipment from trusted suppliers, mobilize skilled personnel and machinery, and establish site facilities. This phase ensures all resources are in place for efficient project execution.

  • Material Procurement
  • Equipment Sourcing
  • Team Mobilization
  • Site Setup
4

Implementation & Construction

Our skilled teams execute the project according to approved plans and specifications. We maintain strict quality control, adhere to safety protocols, and provide regular progress updates to stakeholders throughout the construction phase.

  • Quality Control
  • Safety Management
  • Progress Monitoring
  • Stakeholder Updates
5

Testing, Commissioning & Handover

We conduct comprehensive testing and commissioning to ensure all systems function as designed. This includes performance verification, documentation preparation, client training, and formal project handover with warranty support.

  • System Testing
  • Performance Verification
  • Client Training
  • Project Handover
