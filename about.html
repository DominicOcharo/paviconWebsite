Health & Safety Statement

Safety First

At Pavicon (K) Limited, we are committed to providing a safe and healthy working environment for all our employees, subcontractors, clients, and visitors.

We recognize that health and safety is not only a legal requirement but also a moral obligation. Our goal is to achieve zero accidents and incidents across all our projects and operations.

Our Commitment:
  • Comply with all applicable health and safety legislation, regulations, and industry standards
  • Provide adequate resources, training, and supervision to ensure safe working practices
  • Conduct regular risk assessments and implement appropriate control measures
  • Maintain open communication channels for reporting hazards and safety concerns
  • Continuously improve our health and safety performance through monitoring and review
  • Ensure all personnel are competent and aware of their health and safety responsibilities

Every employee has the right to refuse unsafe work and the responsibility to work safely. Together, we create a culture where safety is everyone's priority.
