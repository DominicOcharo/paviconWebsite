How Renovation Energy Works

Photovoltaic systems, commonly known as Renovation panels, convert sunlight directly into electricity using semiconductors. When sunlight hits the semiconductor material, it knocks electrons loose, creating an electric current. This electricity can be used to power homes, businesses, and even entire communities. PV systems are versatile and can be installed on rooftops, in open fields, and even integrated into building materials.
